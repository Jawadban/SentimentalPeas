Where are we Eating?


Meeting a group of friends for lunch? Deciding where you are going to eat and what kind of food you are going to have can be a challenge!

Let us decide for you:

  1. Provide a location and time. Add the phone numbers of the friends that will be attending.
  2. We will provide a list of restaurants close to your specified location you can choose from based on Yelp reviews. Your friends will vote from these selections.
  3. With the click of a button, your friends will receive a text and can vote!
  4. We will calculate the results and text your friends with the outcome.
  5. Now let's do lunch!
